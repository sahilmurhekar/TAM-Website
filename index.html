About Us

Whether you're an experienced practitioner or a newcomer curious about the possibilities of AI and ML, we invite you to join our vibrant community. Together, let's explore, learn, and innovate as we shape the future of technology. Ready to embark on this journey with us? Get involved and be part of something extraordinary at the AI&ML Club of Vellore Institute of Technology!

WHAT WE OFFER?

  • Workshops and Seminars: Engage in interactive sessions led by industry experts and academic professionals to deepen your understanding of AI and ML concepts.
  • Hands-on Projects: Collaborate with peers on exciting projects that apply AI and ML techniques to solve practical problems and make meaningful contributions.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with professionals, researchers, and fellow students who share your interests and aspirations in the field of AI and ML.

The AI&ML Club is a dynamic student-led organization dedicated to fostering a deep understanding and appreciation for Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning technologies among students at Vellore Institute of Technology, Vellore. Established with the vision of nurturing a community of learners, innovators, and problem solvers, our club serves as a hub for exploring the fascinating intersection of AI and ML with various domains such as healthcare, finance, education, and more.

OUR MISSION

  • To educate and inspire students about the potential of AI and ML through workshops, seminars, and hands-on projects.
  • To facilitate collaboration and knowledge sharing among members to encourage innovative solutions to real-world challenges.
  • To provide a supportive environment where members can enhance their technical skills and pursue their passion for AI and ML.

Events

Glimpses of some of the great events carried out by TAM-VIT

ALGOARENA

Algoarena was a 34-hour AI/ML hackathon at VIT Vellore's YANTRA technical fest. Student teams built solutions to real-world problems, with top projects winning prizes.

MARVELS HACKATHON

Marvels Hackathon was a 34-hour coding challenge at VIT Vellore's GRAVITAS tech fest. Top student projects won prizes and internships, showcasing participant talent and innovation.

DATA ALCHEMY

Data Alchemy was a 2-hour Streamlit workshop at VIT Vellore's GRAVITAS tech fest, teaching students to build and deploy AI/ML apps.

CHATBOTS UNPLUGGED

Chatbots Unplugged was a workshop covering chatbot development, from ideation to deployment. Participants built prototypes and learned techniques for creating intelligent conversational experiences.

Frequently Asked Questions

How to join TAM-VIT?

Every year, VIT students are given the opportunity to enroll in various clubs. When the enrollment period begins, students will receive an email notification. At that time, they can select the 'AI&ML Club' and click 'Final Submission' to complete the enrollment process.

What are the perks to join the club?

Joining the AI&ML Club at VIT comes with numerous perks. Members will get the experience of organizing events and workshops. They will also get to work on various low-level and high-level projects. Additionally, members will get to interact with seniors and alumni, allowing them to strengthen their professional network. Furthermore, the club will provide opportunities to explore many more domains beyond just AI and machine learning.

Does the club limits upto AI&ML only?

The AI & ML Club spans diverse domains - Technical, Management, Design, Outreach, and Editorial. Technical subdomains include IoT, Frontend, Backend, MLOps, Open Source, and AI & ML. Club projects leverage cross-disciplinary expertise.

Are there any prerequisites required to enroll in the club?

There are no prerequisites to join the AI & ML Club. Whether you are a complete beginner, intermediate, or advanced in the field of AI, the club welcomes you. The club will cater to members across skill levels, ensuring everyone can explore and evolve within the dynamic world of artificial intelligence.

How to participate in projects cycles?

The AI & ML Club will circulate a Google form to gather project ideas from members. Top 50 ideas will be shortlisted, and you'll work on them in teams, guided by the board. No prerequisites to join - the club welcomes beginners, intermediates, and advanced learners alike, catering to all skill levels in the dynamic world of AI.
